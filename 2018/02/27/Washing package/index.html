This kind of wrapper can contain tooth-brush toothpaste comb and other disposable daily necessities.You can tear it into two part. One using as a cup,the other can be another package allow you taking the rest of soap back home.Using of this packaging may reduce the waste and protect the environment.
Normal disposable wrapper using on the hotel always waste quite a lot of plastics.Meanwhile,the producing of dixie cups cost a lot of woods too. But our design is mean to make one single package can be used more times.So when you open this package,you don’t need to drop it ,it can be a cup ,an other package a other something else.
Tear the wrapper,you will get the tooth-brush,toothpaste,soap and comb.The lower part can be a cup for brushing.The higher part is a little package that can packaging the rest of soap,make it more easy to take home and go on using. And this wrapper is made from environmental protection material,which can make full use of the resources.
